How to Play

  • You have 6 attempts to guess the word
  • Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word
  • The tile colour changes according to how close your guess was to the word

Example

B
A
D
G
E

B is in the word and in the correct position.

A, D and G are not in the word.

E is in the word but in the wrong position.
